Interaktion

INTERAKTION is a consultancy and coaching company rooted in Denmark, hence the Danish name which means interaction.

We have one core skill:
Building bridges between people, proces and strategy.

Our primary work is on organisational development, corporate communications and leadership development in Denmark as well as abroad.

We naturally see the opportunities and create coherency for both organisation and employees at strategical, tactical and operational levels, especially working cross-functional and multi cultural.
