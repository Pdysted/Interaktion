How to Use
This app is made to help you uncover your true power to hold your ground and not be afraid of change.

It is thought of as a conscious practise where you can explore and reflect upon questions in four group or roles: Host Yourself, Participate, Host Others, and Co-create.



You can explore one role at a time or you can choose a card to answer a question you can hold right there, trusting the serendipity.

Please, explore, learn and enjoy!
